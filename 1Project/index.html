Space Defender

Aliens secretly arrived on Earth and conducted a thorough exploration of our planet and all living beings. They have a mission: they are about to construct a rocket with the intent of promptly informing their race on how to conquer Earth. With their advanced technology, they will complete the rocket in an incredibly short amount of time. Fortunately, the aliens always follow instructions and leave a code for self-destruction... (in case they need to prevent a potentially harmful outcome to their race.) As the most skilled SE on the entire planet, you have been entrusted with the task of uncovering this password RELATED TO JAVASCRIPT, preventing the rocket from flying to Alien Star and giving humanity a chance to survive and thrive...
