Space Defender

Aliens secretly arrived on Earth and conducted a thorough exploration of our planet and all living beings. However, there is the problem: they are about to begun constructing a rocket with the intent of promptly informing their race on how to conquer Earth. With their advanced technology, they will complete the rocket in an incredibly short amount of time. Fortunately, the aliens always follow instructions and always leave a code for self-destruction... As the most skilled linguist on the entire planet, you have been entrusted with the task of uncovering this password, allowing humanity to survive and thrive.

Level: EASY Level: INSANE
